I love to create.


I’ve been working with startups for the past few years. It all began
after my freshman year of college when I took a leave of absence
from college to do marketing for a mobile education app. We went
through the third batch of 500 Startups and it was through this
experience that I realized I wanted to be on the creation end.


When I returned to college the following semester, I started
teaching myself how to program. Since I didn’t have a side project
to work on, I needed another source of motivation. I volunteered to
teach a programming class for beginners and used the date of the
class as my deadline to get down the basics. Through designing
the posters for this class, I found an even greater love—design.


Through working with various teams over the past few years,
I’ve discovered that nothing makes me happier than creating
beautiful experiences and working with a talented team
who believes in their product.
